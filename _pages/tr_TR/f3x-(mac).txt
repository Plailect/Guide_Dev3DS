### Okumanız Gerekli

This is an add-on section for checking your SD card for errors using F3X.

Depending on the size of your SD card and the speed of your computer, this process can take up to several hours!

Bu sayfa yalnızca Mac kullanıcılar içindir. If you are not on Mac, check out the [H2testw (windows)](h2testw-(windows)) or [F3 (Linux)](f3-(linux)) pages.

### What You Need

* The latest release of [F3X](https://github.com/insidegui/F3X/releases)

### Talimatlar

1. F3X `.zip` dosyasını çıkartın
2. Insert your SD card into your computer
2. F3X uygulamasını çalıştırın
4. Hafıza kartınızı seçin
5. "Start Test" 'e basin
5. Wait until the process is complete.

If the test shows the result `Success! Your card is ok!` your SD card is good and you can delete all `.h2w` files on the SD card
{: .notice--success}

If the test shows any other results, your SD card may be corrupted or damaged and you may have to replace it!
{: .notice--danger}
